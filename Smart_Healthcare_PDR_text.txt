Smart Healthcare Monitoring & AI
Consultation Platform
Project Design Report (PDR)
1. Introduction
The Smart Healthcare Monitoring & AI Consultation Platform is a modern healthcare ecosystem designed to simplify
personal health management through AI-assisted recommendations, emergency support systems, and smart
monitoring features.
2. Objectives
The project aims to provide centralized healthcare management, AI-based suggestions, doctor appointment systems,
medicine ordering, emergency healthcare support, and interactive health monitoring.
3. Health Monitoring Dashboard
The dashboard provides weight tracking, heart rate monitoring, ECG animations, health suggestions, reminder
notifications, and charts for better healthcare visualization.
4. Add Health Records
Users can add weight, heart rate, symptoms, and health notes. Records can be edited, deleted, and displayed in
timeline format.
5. AI-Based Health Suggestions
The system analyzes symptoms and recommends precautions and doctor specialists. Example: Fever ® General
Physician, Chest Pain ® Cardiologist.
6. Doctor Appointment System
Users can choose doctors, select online/offline consultations, pick dates and time slots, and book appointments with
confirmation.
7. Medicine Ordering System
The application includes medicine browsing, cart management, address input, order placement, and medicine
delivery simulation.
8. Emergency Support System
The emergency module provides ambulance support, nearby hospital access, emergency contacts, and future
emergency detection logic.
9. Medical Timeline & Reports
Users can upload prescriptions and maintain medical history timelines for better healthcare organization.
10. Technologies Used
Frontend: React.js, React Router, CSS3, Recharts. Backend: Node.js, Express.js. Database: MongoDB Atlas.
11. Future Enhancements
Future upgrades include smartwatch integration, live health tracking, Google Maps hospital finder, AI chatbot
integration, and video consultation support.
12. Conclusion
The Smart Healthcare Platform combines healthcare monitoring, AI recommendations, emergency support, and
medical management into a single interactive ecosystem.
